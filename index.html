About Me

Hello! My name is Giselle Sapiens,an undergraduate student who is passionate about diversity and inclusion in both the dental field and local communities. As a first-generation student, I feel empowered to advocate for my community and others around me to show that ambition and resilience can bring purpose to your life. I am a student attending the University of California, San Diego studying Sociology with a concentration in Science and Medicine. I am an active leader in UCSD Flying Samaritans, a pre-health club lead by students who mission is provide health care and community service to underrepresented individuals in the San Diego. We also take mission trips to our clinic in Enseneda where we provide dental.medical, pharmaceutical,and optical care for the community there I actively participate in providing cloths, food, and hygiene kits to people in Downtown San Diego. Along with UCSD's Flying Samaritans, I am an active member in UCSD's Pre-Dental Society where I assist providing chair-side assistance, clean/sterilize dental instruments, and check in patients. Ultimately, my goal is to become a Dentist to open up a non-profit organization for minority groups, bridging the gap of Latino health disparities and providing care to disadvantaged individuas which I am eager to carry through in succeeding in my pursuit of obtaining my Master's degree.
